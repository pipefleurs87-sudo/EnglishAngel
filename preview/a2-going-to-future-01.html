Going to — Plans, Decisions & Predictions

A2 — grammar, vocabulary, listening, reading, writing

Sofía: I have plans! I'm going to redesign my studio this weekend.

Ben: We're going on a date on Saturday... I mean, I'm going to ask her first.

Kenji: Look at those clouds. It's going to rain on your date, Ben.

Abuela Carmen: And Ben is going to forget his umbrella. That's not a prediction — that's experience.

Common mistake: “I going to study tonight.” — Correct: “I'm going to study tonight.”

We use 'going to' for plans and decisions already made, and for predictions when you can see the evidence — dark clouds, a losing team.

Practice

  2. Look at those clouds! It ___ rain. (is going to, go to, going to)
  3. We're going ___ a date on the weekend. (on, to, at)
  4. We use 'going to' for plans and decisions we already made.
  5. 'I going to study' is correct — the verb 'be' is not necessary.
  6. Sofía ___ (be) going to travel to Cartagena next month.
  7. We ___ (not / be) going to cook tonight; we're going to order pizza.

Listening

Sofía: What are you going to do this weekend?

Ben: I'm going on a date on Saturday. We're going to have a picnic in the park.

Kenji: A picnic? Look at the sky, Ben. It's going to rain!

Ben: No, no. The app says it's going to be sunny.

Abuela Carmen: Ben, my knee hurts. That means it's going to rain. Take an umbrella.

Ben: OK, OK... I'm going to take the umbrella!

Reading

Sofía loves plans. Every Sunday night, she writes her list for the week. This week is special: 'On Monday, I'm going to present my new design to a client from Bogotá. On Wednesday, Kenji and I are going to visit a photography exhibition. On Friday, I'm not going to work late — I'm going to have dinner with Abuela Carmen. And on Saturday... Ben and his date are going to need help, so we're all going to wait near the park. Just in case.' At the bottom of the list, there is one more line: 'Prediction: Ben is going to be fine. Probably.'

  • Sofía is going to present a design on Monday.
  • She is going to work late on Friday.
  • Kenji and Sofía are going to visit a science museum.
  • Sofía predicts that Ben is going to be fine.
