There is / There are — Describing the House

A2 — grammar, vocabulary, listening, reading, writing

Sofía: In my apartment there is a small studio where I design. There are plants everywhere!

Ben: In my hostel room there is one bed... and there isn't a window. Classic Ben luck.

Kenji: Are there many rooms in your house? In Japan, my family's house is very small.

Abuela Carmen: In my old house there was a garden with mango trees. Now there is a parking lot there...

Common mistake: “There is two bedrooms in my house.” — Correct: “There are two bedrooms in my house.”

We use 'there is' and 'there are' to say what exists in a place — one form for singular things and one for plural, unlike Spanish 'hay'.

there is / there are
SingularPlural
AffirmativeThere is a sofa / an ovenThere are two beds
NegativeThere isn't a garageThere aren't any plants
QuestionIs there a shower?Are there any windows?
Short answerYes, there is. / No, there isn't.Yes, there are. / No, there aren't.
Note: an + vowel SOUNDan oven, an elevator, an armchaira window, a garden (consonant sound)

Practice

  1. ___ two bedrooms in my apartment. (There are, There is, There am)
  2. There is ___ oven in the kitchen. (an, a, two)
  3. Is there a garage? ___ (Yes, there is., Yes, it is., Yes, there are.)
  4. We use 'there are' with plural nouns: 'there are two beds'.
  5. We choose 'an' by the sound, not the letter: 'an oven' but 'a window'.
  6. There ___ a big sofa in the living room.
  7. There ___ four chairs in the kitchen.
  8. When Abuela Carmen was young, there ___ no supermarkets in her town.
  9. Unscramble: there, is, a, mirror, in, the, bathroom
  10. Unscramble: are, there, two, bedrooms, in, your, house
  11. Find the mistake: There is two bedrooms in my house.
  12. Find the mistake: There are a garden behind the house.
  13. Find the mistake: In the kitchen there is a oven.
  14. There is a garage. — Write the negative form.
  15. There are two windows in the bedroom. — Write the yes/no question.
  16. Is there a shower in the bathroom? — Answer with a short answer (Yes).
  17. There is a TV in the bedroom. — Write the negative form.
  18. There aren't any stairs in the house. — Write the affirmative form (use 'some').
  19. Is there a garden in your house? (short answer)
  20. Are there five bathrooms in your house? (short answer)

Listening

Sofía: Kenji, how is your new house?

Kenji: It's great! There are three bedrooms and there is a beautiful garden.

Ben: Is there a garage?

Kenji: Yes, there is. But there isn't a car — my host family rides bicycles!

Sofía: Are there any plants in your room?

Kenji: No, there aren't. But there is a big window with a view of the mountains.
