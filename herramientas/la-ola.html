La Ola

one wave. one phone. ten grammars.

Ben Whitfield — British backpacker, professionally clumsy — took his phone into the sea in Cartagena. A wave took it back. Those 30 seconds echo through ten different B2 topics on this site: same story, different grammar lens each time. Scroll the constellation.
